Indie Author

Some people might not see this as a skill, but I can say from experience that it is. Publishing books on the internet doesn't just involve writing the novel. When I first started, I just published my books and did nothing else, and it felt like I was screaming into the void. To be even a modestly successful indie author, you have to be your own marketing team. You have to have the dedication to stick with it even when you're just seeing crickets in the beginning. Since 2020, I dedicated myself to gathering an audience for my books. I've studied and evolved to gain an audience and get my book out there. I would like to humbly say that my efforts have paid off.
